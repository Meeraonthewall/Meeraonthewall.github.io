About US

Welcome to the Caribbean Organization. We are about our Caribbean culture and roots. Through activities including culinary tastings, music and dance performances, cultural festivals, and customary holidays like Carnival and Emancipation Day, the group promotes understanding and respect of Caribbean culture. It seeks to inform others about the contributions, history, and customs of the Caribbean people. Workshops, outside speakers, language instruction (such as in Creole or Patois), and educational excursions are a few examples of this. The group offers a forum for community members and Caribbean students to interact, exchange stories, and offer mutual support. It might provide peer support groups, networking opportunities, and mentorship programs to help with both career and personal development. Frequent social gatherings like movie evenings, themed parties, and meet-and-greets promote community development and provide members a chance to connect over common interests.
